Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro (born 5 February 1985), commonly known as CR7, is a Portuguese professional footballer who currently captains Al-Nassr in the Saudi Pro League and the Portugal national team. Widely regarded as one of the greatest players in history, he has scored over 960 official senior career goals across more than 1,300 appearances, making him the top goalscorer of all time.
